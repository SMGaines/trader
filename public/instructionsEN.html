TRADER v1.0
An online trading game for 2-8 players.

Introduction
  • Players start with $100,000 and have to buy and sell shares on the stock market to maximise their profit.
  • The winner is the person who has the highest Net Worth (Cash + Shares value) at the end of the game
  • The game lasts for two 'years' and every month, an 'event' occurs such as a 'boom' or 'crash' or even a lottery win! (see full list below).
  • Skillfully reacting to these events is the key to success!
  • Insider Trading allows you to get secret information about the Stock Market ahead of time - but don't get caught or you'll go to prison!
  • Watch out for other players 'hacking' your account to steal your cash!
  • If you go bankrupt, you're out of the game!
Stocks
There are four stocks that can be traded :-
  • GOVT - very safe and reliable but profits can be slow in coming
  • GOLD - Safe and prices can occasionally rise fast
  • OIL - Pretty safe but can be sometimes affected by events
  • HITECH - Very volatile - expect big gains..or losses
Player Actions
  • Buy: You can buy shares (in blocks of 50) of a particular stock if you have enough cash.
  • Sell: You can sell shares (in blocks of 50) of a particular stock that you already own
  • Hack: You can 'hack' other players in order to steal cash. If you get caught, you'll be fined and go to prison!
  • Suspect: If you think someone is hacking you, you can 'suspect' them. If you're right, you'll receive compensation. If wrong, you will be fined
  • Insider trading: Players can get information ahead of time about what will happen in the markets. If you get caught, you'll go to prison
NOTE: If your cash ever gets below $0, all of your shares will automatically be sold to pay your debts. If your cash is still below $0, you will become bankrupt and you are out of the game.
Interest & Inflation
  • Interest Rates: Government interest rates rise and fall during the game. Interest rates increase your cash constantly through the game
  • Inflation: Inflation rises and falls during the game. Inflation reduces your cash constantly through the game.
Events during the game
Once a 'month' events occur during the game :-
  • Interest rate change - Interest rates can go up or down
  • Inflation rate change - Inflation can go up or down
  • Tax Return - Every December, Tax Returns are due where you pay 20% tax on all the shares you own
  • Lottery Win - A random player will receive a cash prize
  • Stock Market Crash - all stock prices, apart from GOVT, fall dramatically
  • Stock Market Boom - all stock prices, apart from GOVT, rise dramatically
  • Stock Crash - one particular stock crashes
  • Stock Boom - one particular stock booms
  • Stock IPO - a new stock is released to the market
  • Stock Release - extra shares of a particular stock are available to buy
  • Stock Dividend - you receive extra shares of a particular stock - if you already own at least 50 shares of this stock
  • Stock Suspended - a stock is suspended from being traded. The price goes to $0 and you cannot buy or sell this stock until the suspension is lifted
